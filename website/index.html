What We Offer

AI Battle Buddies

Chat with our understanding AI companions who are always ready to listen, day or night.

Our AI Battle Buddies are available 24/7 and understand military culture. Each has a unique personality — from Tommy's friendly chat to Bob's straight-talking Para banter. They're trained to listen without judgment, offer coping strategies, and know when to guide you to human support. Perfect for those 3am moments when you need someone to talk to.

Peer Support

Connect with trained peer supporters who understand your military experience.

Our peer supporters are veterans and serving personnel who've walked in your boots. They've completed specialist training in active listening and mental health first aid. Whether you need a brew and a chat, advice on transitioning to civvy street, or just someone who gets it — they're here for you. No judgment, no paperwork, just real support from people who understand.

Crisis Counselling

Access professional counsellors when you need specialised support.

When things get tough, our qualified counsellors are available for voice calls through the app. They specialise in military-related issues including PTSD, transition challenges, and relationship difficulties. Conversations are confidential, and you can request a callback at a time that suits you. We also connect you with specialist services like Combat Stress when needed.

Self-Care Tools

Journaling, breathing exercises, grounding techniques, and more.

Your personal toolkit for mental wellness. Private journaling to process your thoughts, guided breathing exercises for anxiety and panic, grounding techniques for flashbacks and difficult moments, and sleep hygiene tips for those restless nights. All stored locally on your device — your private space for reflection and recovery.

Buddy Finder

Find and reconnect with veterans in your area or from your unit.

Missing that sense of belonging? Buddy Finder helps you connect with other veterans in your area or find old mates from your regiment. Search by location, service branch, or unit. Build your local support network, organise meetups, or simply know you're not alone. Because sometimes the best support comes from those who served alongside you.

Support Directory

Browse UK veteran charities and support organisations.

A comprehensive directory of UK veteran charities, regimental associations, and support services. From housing help to employment support, legal advice to family services — we've gathered the resources so you don't have to search. Filter by what you need, read about each organisation, and connect directly. Help is out there; we just make it easier to find.
