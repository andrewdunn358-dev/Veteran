What is Radio Check?

Radio Check is a free, completely confidential mental health and peer support service built by veterans, for the UK Armed Forces community. Whether you're currently serving, a veteran, a reservist, or a family member - we're here for you, day or night.

No Names Required

Completely anonymous. No registration, no records, no impact on your service record or security clearance.

Available 24/7

Our AI Battle Buddies are always online. Human counsellors and peer supporters available during staffed hours.

Built By Veterans

Created by people who've served, who understand military culture, and who get it.

How Radio Check Helps

Whether you're struggling right now or just want someone to talk to, we offer multiple ways to get support.

AI Battle Buddies

9 AI companions with unique personalities, available 24/7 for a chat whenever you need it.

Our AI Battle Buddies understand military culture and speak your language. From Tommy's laid-back chat to Bob's straight-talking Para banter, Frankie's PTI fitness motivation to Rachel's warm, caring support - there's someone for every mood. They're trained to listen without judgment, offer coping strategies, and know when to guide you to human support. Perfect for those 3am moments.

Live Chat Support

Text chat with real counsellors and peer supporters who understand military life.

Connect instantly via text chat with trained peer supporters and qualified counsellors. Our team includes veterans from all three services who've completed specialist mental health training. No phone calls needed - just type when you're ready. Conversations are encrypted and confidential. Request a callback if you'd prefer to talk voice-to-voice.

Callback Service

Request a call from a counsellor at a time that suits you.

Prefer to talk? Request a callback and one of our counsellors will ring you at your chosen time. Our counsellors specialise in military-related issues including PTSD, transition challenges, moral injury, and relationship difficulties. Calls are made from private numbers and conversations remain confidential.

The Gym

Frankie's 12-week PTI fitness programme to build strength and mental resilience.

Physical fitness supports mental health. Frankie, our AI PTI, delivers a progressive 12-week programme designed for all fitness levels. Proper military-style PT with warm-ups, main sessions, and cool-downs. Track your progress, get motivated, and feel the benefits of structured exercise. No gym membership needed - all bodyweight exercises you can do anywhere.

Self-Care Tools

Private journaling, breathing exercises, grounding techniques, and sleep tips.

Your personal mental wellness toolkit. Private journaling to process thoughts and track your mood. Guided breathing exercises for anxiety and panic attacks. Grounding techniques for flashbacks and difficult moments. Sleep hygiene tips for restless nights. Everything stored locally on your device - your private space for reflection and recovery.

Support Directory

Comprehensive database of UK veteran charities and military support services.

Finding help shouldn't be hard. Our directory covers housing, employment, legal advice, family support, addiction services, and more. Each listing includes contact details, what they offer, and eligibility criteria. Filter by what you need and connect directly. We've done the research so you don't have to.

Who Is Radio Check For?

Radio Check is for everyone in the UK Armed Forces community. No matter your rank, service, or situation.

Serving Personnel

Currently serving in the Regular Forces or Reserves? We're here for you. Completely confidential - no impact on your career, security clearance, or medical records.

Veterans

Whether you served 4 years or 24, left last month or decades ago - Radio Check supports you through transition and beyond. You never stop being part of the forces family.

Families

Partners, parents, children, and loved ones of serving personnel and veterans. Rita, our family support AI, specialises in helping those who support others.

Why "Radio Check"?

In military communications, a "Radio Check" is a simple request to confirm someone's listening - to know you're not alone on the net.

That's what this app is about. When you're struggling at 3am, when the thoughts won't stop, when you need to talk but don't know who to call - Radio Check is your confirmation that someone's listening. You're not broadcasting into the void.

"Radio Check" - because sometimes you just need to know someone's there.
