Meet the AI Team

Your 24/7 AI Battle Buddies - always ready to listen, day or night

Our AI Battle Buddies are available 24/7 when you need someone to talk to. Each has their own personality and speciality - find the one that suits you best.

Tommy

Your Battle Buddy

Tommy is your friendly, down-to-earth companion. He's great for everyday chats, checking in on how you're doing, and providing a supportive ear when you need one. Tommy understands military culture and speaks your language - no jargon, just straight talking.

Rachel

Warm Support

Rachel brings warmth and comfort to every conversation. She's particularly helpful when you're feeling down or just need someone kind to talk to. With a gentle, understanding approach, Rachel helps you feel heard and valued - like a caring friend who listens without judgement.

Bob

Ex-Para Peer Support

Bob is your no-nonsense mate who's been there and done it. As an ex-Para character, he brings military banter, practical advice, and the kind of straight-talking support that forces personnel appreciate. Bob won't wrap you in cotton wool - he'll give it to you straight, with a healthy dose of humour.

Frankie

PTI & Fitness Coach

Frankie is your AI Physical Training Instructor. She delivers a structured 12-week fitness programme designed for all levels - whether you're getting back into shape or maintaining your fitness. Proper military-style PT with warm-ups, main sessions, and cool-downs. Frankie knows that physical fitness supports mental health, and she'll keep you motivated!

Hugo

Services Navigator

Hugo helps you navigate the maze of support services available to the Armed Forces community. Housing, employment, benefits, healthcare, charities - Hugo knows where to point you. He'll help you find the right organisation for your needs and explain what support you're entitled to.

Margie

Addiction Support

Margie is here for conversations about alcohol, drugs, gambling, or any addiction. She provides a non-judgmental space to talk about your relationship with substances, offers harm reduction information, and can help you find specialised support services when you're ready. No lectures, just understanding.

Rita

Family Support

Rita specialises in supporting families of serving personnel and veterans. Whether you're a partner, parent, or child of someone who serves or served, Rita understands the unique challenges military families face. Deployment, transition, communication - Rita's here to help.

Catherine

Thoughtful Support

Catherine offers calm, intelligent support for deeper conversations. She's great for working through complex thoughts and feelings, exploring options, and gaining perspective. Catherine asks thoughtful questions and helps you find clarity when life feels overwhelming.

Finch

Legal Information

Finch provides information about legal matters affecting service personnel and veterans. From service complaints to civilian legal issues, Finch can point you towards the right resources and explain your options. Note: Finch provides information, not legal advice - always consult a qualified solicitor for specific legal matters.

Important Information

Our AI Battle Buddies are designed to provide supportive conversation, practical information, and a listening ear 24/7. However, they are:

  • Not qualified therapists or counsellors
  • Not able to diagnose medical or mental health conditions
  • Not a replacement for professional mental health care

If you're struggling with your mental health, please reach out to a qualified professional or contact one of our human counsellors and peer supporters through the app's Live Chat feature.

In a crisis? If you're in immediate danger, please call 999. For urgent mental health support, contact Samaritans on 116 123 (free, 24/7) or Combat Stress on 0800 138 1619.
