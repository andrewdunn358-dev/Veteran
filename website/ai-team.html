Meet the AI Team

Your 24/7 AI Battle Buddies, always ready to listen

Tommy

Your Battle Buddy

Tommy is your friendly, down-to-earth companion. He's great for everyday chats, checking in on how you're doing, and providing a supportive ear when you need one. Tommy understands military culture and speaks your language — no jargon, just straight talking.

Doris

Warm Support

Doris brings warmth and comfort to every conversation. She's particularly helpful when you're feeling down or just need someone kind to talk to. With a gentle, understanding approach, Doris helps you feel heard and valued, just like a caring friend would.

Bob

Ex-Para Peer Support

Bob is your no-nonsense mate who's been there and done it. As an ex-Para character, he brings military banter, practical advice, and the kind of straight-talking support that veterans appreciate. Bob won't wrap you in cotton wool — he'll give it to you straight, with a healthy dose of humour.

Finch

Crisis & PTSD Support

Finch specialises in supporting those dealing with PTSD, trauma, and difficult moments. When things get tough, Finch provides grounding techniques, breathing exercises, and a calm, steady presence. Finch is trained to recognise when you might need additional help and can guide you to human support.

Margie

Alcohol & Substance Support

Margie is here for conversations about alcohol and substance use. She provides a non-judgmental space to talk about your relationship with substances, offers harm reduction information, and can help you find specialised support services when you're ready. No lectures, just understanding.

Hugo

Self-Help & Wellness

Hugo is your wellness guide, focusing on self-improvement, mindfulness, and positive habits. Whether you want to establish better routines, learn relaxation techniques, or just feel more balanced, Hugo provides practical tips and encouragement to help you on your journey to feeling better.

Important Information

Our AI companions are designed to provide supportive conversation and practical information. However, they are not:

  • Qualified therapists or counsellors
  • Able to diagnose medical or mental health conditions
  • A replacement for professional mental health care

If you're struggling with your mental health, please reach out to a qualified professional or contact one of our human peer supporters through the app.
