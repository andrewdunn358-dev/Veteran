## Setup Instructions

### WordPress + LearnDash Setup:

1. **Install WordPress** on your hosting
2. **Install Astra Theme** (free version is fine)
3. **Install Plugins:**
   - LearnDash LMS (paid - ~$199/year)
   - Elementor (free)
   - LearnDash Certificate Builder (included with LearnDash)
   
4. **Apply Custom CSS:**
   - Go to: Appearance > Customize > Additional CSS
   - Paste contents of `radio-check-theme.css`
   
5. **Upload Logo:**
   - Go to: Appearance > Customize > Site Identity
   - Upload the Radio Check logo

## Course Structure: 10 Modules

### Module 1: Introduction to Peer Support
**Duration:** 45 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand what peer support is and isn't
- Recognise the value of lived experience
- Understand the role boundaries of a peer supporter

**Content:**
1. Welcome video (5 min)
2. What is Peer Support?
   - Definition and principles
   - The power of shared experience
   - Difference from counselling/therapy
3. The Peer Supporter Role
   - What you WILL do: listen, support, signpost
   - What you WON'T do: advise, diagnose, treat
4. Why Peer Support Works for Veterans
   - Military culture understanding
   - Breaking down barriers
   - Trust through shared experience
5. Workbook: Self-reflection exercise
6. **Quiz:** 10 questions (70% pass mark)

---

### Module 2: BACP Ethical Framework
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand the core BACP ethical principles
- Apply ethical thinking to peer support scenarios
- Recognise ethical dilemmas

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (5 min)
2. The Four Ethical Principles:
   - **Being Trustworthy** - Honouring commitments, reliability
   - **Autonomy** - Respecting the person's right to self-governance
   - **Beneficence** - Working for the good of the client
   - **Non-maleficence** - Avoiding harm
3. Personal Moral Qualities:
   - Empathy
   - Sincerity
   - Integrity
   - Resilience
   - Respect
   - Humility
   - Competence
   - Fairness
   - Wisdom
   - Courage
4. Case Studies: Ethical scenarios
5. Workbook: Ethical decision-making framework
6. **Quiz:** 15 questions (75% pass mark)

---

### Module 3: Active Listening Skills
**Duration:** 75 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Master reflective listening techniques
- Practice empathic responding
- Avoid common listening pitfalls

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (5 min)
2. The Art of Listening
   - Hearing vs. listening vs. understanding
   - Creating space for the other person
3. Reflective Listening Techniques:
   - Paraphrasing
   - Summarising
   - Reflecting feelings
   - Open questions
4. Barriers to Effective Listening:
   - Interrupting
   - Advising too quickly
   - Relating everything to yourself
   - Judging
   - Mind-wandering
5. Non-Verbal Communication:
   - Eye contact (in person)
   - Tone of voice (on phone)
   - Silence as a tool
6. Video demonstrations (15 min)
7. Workbook: Practice exercises
8. **Quiz:** 12 questions (70% pass mark)

---

### Module 4: Understanding Trauma & PTSD
**Duration:** 90 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand the nature of trauma
- Recognise PTSD symptoms
- Respond appropriately to trauma disclosures

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (10 min)
2. What is Trauma?
   - Definition and types
   - Military-specific trauma
   - Moral injury
3. Understanding PTSD:
   - Intrusion symptoms (flashbacks, nightmares)
   - Avoidance
   - Negative alterations in mood/cognition
   - Arousal and reactivity changes
4. Complex PTSD:
   - Emotional dysregulation
   - Negative self-concept
   - Relationship difficulties
5. The Window of Tolerance
   - Hyperarousal vs. hypoarousal
   - Helping someone regulate
6. Responding to Disclosures:
   - What to say and not say
   - Avoiding re-traumatisation
   - Grounding techniques
7. Workbook: Trauma-informed responses
8. **Quiz:** 15 questions (75% pass mark)

---

### Module 5: Boundaries & Self-Care
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Establish healthy boundaries
- Recognise signs of burnout
- Develop personal self-care strategies

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (5 min)
2. Why Boundaries Matter:
   - Protecting yourself
   - Protecting the person you support
   - Maintaining effectiveness
3. Types of Boundaries:
   - Time boundaries
   - Emotional boundaries
   - Role boundaries
   - Physical boundaries
4. Setting Boundaries in Practice:
   - How to say no
   - Managing expectations
   - When to step back
5. Vicarious Trauma & Burnout:
   - Warning signs
   - Risk factors
   - Prevention strategies
6. Self-Care Strategies:
   - Physical self-care
   - Emotional self-care
   - Professional self-care
   - Supervision and support
7. Workbook: Personal self-care plan
8. **Quiz:** 10 questions (70% pass mark)

---

### Module 6: Confidentiality & Safeguarding
**Duration:** 75 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand confidentiality principles
- Know when confidentiality must be broken
- Follow safeguarding procedures

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (5 min)
2. Confidentiality Principles:
   - What is covered
   - How to explain to those you support
   - Data protection basics (GDPR)
3. Limits to Confidentiality:
   - Risk to self
   - Risk to others
   - Safeguarding concerns
   - Legal requirements
4. Safeguarding Adults:
   - Types of abuse
   - Signs and indicators
   - Reporting procedures
5. Working with Radio Check:
   - Record keeping requirements
   - Who to escalate to
   - Using the panic button
6. Case Studies: When to break confidentiality
7. Workbook: Safeguarding scenarios
8. **Quiz:** 15 questions (80% pass mark) - CRITICAL MODULE

---

### Module 7: Recognising Risk & Crisis
**Duration:** 90 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Identify suicide risk factors
- Respond safely to crisis disclosures
- Know escalation pathways

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (10 min)
2. Understanding Suicide:
   - Myths vs. facts
   - Risk factors in veterans
   - Protective factors
3. Warning Signs:
   - Verbal indicators
   - Behavioural indicators
   - Situational factors
4. Asking About Suicide:
   - How to ask directly
   - What to do with the answer
   - Safety planning basics
5. Crisis Response:
   - Staying calm
   - Keeping the person talking
   - When to call emergency services (999)
6. Using Radio Check Escalation:
   - When to use the panic button
   - Contacting counsellors
   - Documentation requirements
7. After a Crisis:
   - Debriefing
   - Self-care after difficult calls
   - Learning from experiences
8. Video demonstrations (20 min)
9. Workbook: Crisis response practice
10. **Quiz:** 20 questions (85% pass mark) - CRITICAL MODULE

---

### Module 8: Communication Skills
**Duration:** 60 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Communicate effectively by phone, text, and video
- Handle difficult conversations
- Manage challenging behaviours

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (5 min)
2. Phone Communication:
   - Building rapport without visuals
   - Managing silences
   - Ending calls appropriately
3. Text/Message Communication:
   - Tone in writing
   - Response timing
   - Limitations of text support
4. Video Call Communication:
   - Technical considerations
   - Maintaining connection
   - Privacy and background
5. Difficult Conversations:
   - Anger and frustration
   - Repeated crises
   - Dependency issues
   - When someone doesn't want to talk
6. Managing Your Own Emotions:
   - Staying regulated
   - Taking breaks
   - Seeking support
7. Workbook: Communication scenarios
8. **Quiz:** 12 questions (70% pass mark)

---

### Module 9: Signposting & Resources
**Duration:** 45 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Know key support services
- Make appropriate referrals
- Support transitions to other services

**Content:**
1. Introduction video (5 min)
2. The Radio Check Ecosystem:
   - Peer supporters
   - Counsellors
   - Partner organisations
3. Key National Services:
   - Combat Stress (0800 138 1619)
   - SSAFA
   - Royal British Legion
   - Help for Heroes
   - Walking With The Wounded
4. NHS Mental Health Services:
   - Op COURAGE
   - GP referral pathway
   - Crisis teams
5. Emergency Services:
   - When to call 999
   - What to tell them
6. Making Effective Referrals:
   - Warm handoffs
   - Following up
   - Managing expectations
7. Workbook: Resource directory
8. **Quiz:** 10 questions (70% pass mark)

---

### Module 10: Practical Assessment
**Duration:** 120 minutes
**Learning Objectives:**
- Demonstrate competence in all areas
- Apply learning to realistic scenarios
- Receive feedback and certification

**Content:**
1. Assessment Overview (10 min video)
2. Written Scenarios (60 min):
   - 5 case studies requiring written responses
   - Covers ethics, boundaries, risk, and communication
   - Marked by assessor
3. Role Play Recording (30 min):
   - Record yourself responding to a mock call
   - Upload for assessor review
4. Final Exam (30 min):
   - 40 multiple choice questions
   - Covers all modules
   - **Pass mark: 80%**
5. Reflection Piece:
   - 500-word reflection on learning journey
   - Personal development goals

**Certification Requirements:**
- Complete all 10 modules
- Pass all quizzes
- Pass final exam (80%+)
- Submit role play recording
- Submit reflection piece

## Recommended LearnDash Settings

1. **Course Access:** Closed (admin enrollment or purchase)
2. **Lesson Timer:** Enabled (prevent skipping)
3. **Video Progression:** Required (must watch full video)
4. **Quiz Settings:**
   - No retakes for critical modules (6, 7, 10)
   - 3 retakes for other modules
   - Randomise question order
5. **Certificate:** Auto-generated on completion
6. **Expiry:** 2 years (with renewal course available)
